Alfajores

Alfajores are a type of traditional South American cookie, popular in countries like Argentina, Peru, and Uruguay. They typically consist of two soft, crumbly biscuits filled with a sweet filling, usually dulce de leche (a caramel-like milk spread). The cookies are often coated with powdered sugar.

Angel Food Cake

Angel food cake is a light and airy sponge cake made primarily from egg whites, sugar, and flour. It is known for its fluffy texture and is typically baked in a tube pan, which helps the cake rise and maintain its height. The absence of butter or oil gives angel food cake its characteristic lightness.

Kulfi

Kulfi is a traditional Indian frozen dessert, similar to ice cream but denser and creamier. It is made by slowly simmering full-fat milk until it reduces significantly, which gives it a rich, concentrated flavor and a slightly caramelized taste. The thickened milk is then sweetened, flavored, and frozen in molds.
